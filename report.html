Key Findings

L1 Structured Extraction Outperforms L3 Autonomous Reasoning

On RuleArena Airline Level 0, L1 achieves 80% accuracy while L3 achieves 0%. L1 is also 1.6× cheaper ($0.015 vs $0.024) and 3.4× faster (68s vs 232s). This supports the distillation hypothesis: decomposing into extraction + deterministic calculation yields more reliable results than end-to-end LLM reasoning. The 20% L1 errors are extraction failures—debuggable and potentially fixable with better prompting.
